What does PID stand for? What is the purpose of the P, the I, and the D? What steps did you take to choose the best values for your robot? Explain this like you would for someone taking this course next year.

  PID stands for Proportional, Integral, Derivative. The purpose of PID is to take sensor values and accurately convert them into motion for your robot. P goes back and forth proportionately to the error, I takes error over time and D takes change in error over time.
